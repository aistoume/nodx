What nodx does differently

🪜 First-principles decomposition

The AI asks the unsolved questions, doesn't dispense the answer. You stay in control of which paths to deepen.

🎙 Expert-panel protocol

For each direction, multiple AI experts (with a mandatory devil's advocate) run a structured 4-round debate until reaching a Local Maximum conclusion.

📌 Annotations that persist

Every explanation, note, and atomic action stays anchored to the text that triggered it — survives close, returns on click.

🧠 Thinking library (CBR)

Your past completed decisions become an indexed, searchable case library. New questions can fork-and-adapt from old ones instead of being thought from scratch.

🚀 Auto-recursion (preview)

Don't stop at "here are some ideas." A Project Manager AI keeps spawning sub-discussions until the conclusion is concretely actionable — with you in the loop at every step.

🔐 Local-first by default

Your thinking lives on your machine. Bring-your-own API key. No server collects what you reason about.
