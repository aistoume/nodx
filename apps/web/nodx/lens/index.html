What makes it different

🔍 Select-to-explain

Highlight 2–500 characters → click 🔍 → streamed explanation in place. 50–150 words by default; tap "Deepen" for the long version.

📌 Persistent annotations

Every explanation leaves a blue underline that survives your panel close. Click it later to re-open the same explanation — no extra API call.

🌊 Real streaming

Words appear as the model generates them. Tell within the first sentence whether the answer is on-track — close early if it isn't.

🔐 Privacy by design

No nodx server. Your API key, settings, and history stay in chrome.storage.local. Requests go directly from your browser to your AI provider.

🌐 Bilingual

UI in 中文 or English. The AI itself responds in whatever language you've set — perfect for reading across languages.

🧰 Bring your own key

Anthropic Claude, OpenAI GPT, or Google Gemini — pick your model, pick your speed, pay only for what you use. No subscription lock-in.

How it works

  1. Install from the Chrome Web Store (60 seconds).
  2. Open Settings — pick a provider, paste your own API key.
  3. Read normally — Gemini, ChatGPT, Substack, papers, anywhere.
  4. Highlight a phrase — the 🔍 trigger floats above your selection.
  5. Click → read — explanation streams below; underline stays for later.
