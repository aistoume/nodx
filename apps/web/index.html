How we work

Ship small. Ship often.

We ship deliberately scoped tools quickly and let real use teach us what to build next. Less roadmap, more contact with reality.

Augment, don't replace.

The best AI tools push back. We're suspicious of "one click and done" because the work that matters is the work of choosing.

Local-first, data-respectful.

Our products default to bring-your-own-key and on-device storage. We make money from tools you pay for, not from data you didn't realize you gave away.
